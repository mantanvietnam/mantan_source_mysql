Album Template

This wedding photo album template for Photoshop will allow you to insert your wedding photos, edit the wedding date, bride and groom’s names and even edit the quotes with anything you’d like it to say through fully editable text layers. All the colors can be adjusted easily and if 12×12 isn’t your desired size, you can simply scale it up or down to any size wedding album you like without loss of quality.

There are 10 spreads included in this wedding photo album template, which means there are 20 pages in total with collages showing the best shots from your wedding. A cover template is also included to put a beautiful photo of your wedding on the front and write the bride and groom’s name and wedding date underneath.

This wedding album template is made using the print requirements of WHCC, but can also be printed with other print labs.
