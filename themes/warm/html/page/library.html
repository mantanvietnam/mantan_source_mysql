Dien Bien project factsheet

Due to climate change, the historical and touristic city of Dien Bien Phu in Dien Bien province in Northwest Vietnam, is more exposed to flooding from the Nam Rom River. AFD and the European Union are supporting the city to enhance the flood risk management capacity.
