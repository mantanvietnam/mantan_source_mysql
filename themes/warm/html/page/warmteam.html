Warm team

WARM Facility is implemented in Vietnam by Agence Française de Développement Group (AFD Vietnam) with the support of the Government of Vietnam.

AFD is a public financial institution that finances, supports and accelerates transitions towards a more sustainable world. AFD has financed in Vietnam over 90 projects since 1994 for a total of almost 2.1 billion euros in different sectors: transport, infrastructure, energy and agriculture. In line with the priorities of French cooperation in Vietnam, AFD is positioning its operations to support the implementation of the Paris Agreement (https://www.afd.fr/).

AFD has been operating in Vietnam since 1994 and has financed over 90 projects worth almost EUR 2.1 billion in various sectors such as climate change, transport, energy and infrastructure. In line with the priorities of French cooperation with Vietnam, AFD is positioning its operations to support the implementation of the Paris Agreement.

For the WARM project, AFD has deployed a dedicated team in Vietnam to successfully guide, coordinate, supervise and implement the works with the various government agencies. AFD team is supported by a consortium (AETS and MKE) under a consultant contract, especially in terms of coordination of different components and crosscutting activities.
